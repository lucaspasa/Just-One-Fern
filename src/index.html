OUR HOSTS

Aaron Fern

Aaron Fern is a multi-culteral vagabond with a knack for 'hanging loose'. Although he is known primarily as the voice actor for the animated TV character Brian Griffen from the hit show Family Guy, he often is found watching birds and aiming his BB Gun at them yet never shooting, no never, he promised himself he wouldn't become his father.

  • Tully's Good Times,

  • Dislikes:

Lucas Rinaldi

Lucas Rinaldi is a musician based in Brooklyn, New York. If he isn't playing chicken with his to-do list he is often found thinking of new ways to annoy Aaron. Lucas' other big creative project is Land, an Indie Rock band. If need be, you can contact him at his desolate instagram account, which he may never update.
