JUST ONE
FERN

A comedy podcast by musicians

Hosts Aaron Fern and Lucas Rinaldi interview independent musicians and comedians, while also discussing their lives as independent artists!

"A couple of guys talking about a whole lot of nothing"

OUR HOSTS

Aaron Fern

Aaron Fern is a musician and god killer from Nashville, Tenessee and is a devout Syracuse basketball fan. He spends his days working for Warner Music Group and his nights bartending at everyones favorite brewery "The Dirty Ferny". One day Aaron hopes to serve chicken noodle soup to an ill Justin Timberlake. Justin, if you're out there don't be afraid to give us a shout.

Lucas Rinaldi

Lucas Rinaldi is a musician based in Brooklyn, New York. If he isn't playing chicken with his to-do list he is often found thinking of new ways to annoy Aaron. Lucas' other big creative project is Citrus Maxima, an Indie Rock band. If need be, you can contact him at his desolate instagram account, which he may never update.
